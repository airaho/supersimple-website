Watching Anime

Demon Slayer

Plot: A boy becomes a Demon Slayer to avenge his family and save his demon turned-sister.

What's Cool: Beautiful animation, emotional story, and intense sword fights.

Attack on Titan

Plot: Humanity fights giant Titans, uncovering dark secrets along the way.

What's Cool: Epic plot twists, complex characters, and deep political themes.

Code Breaker

Plot: A girl meets a vigilante who kills with fire, sparking debates about justice.

What's Cool: Superpowers, moral conflicts, and government conspiracies.

The Devil Is a Part-Timer

Plot: A demon lord is stuck in modern-day Tokyo, working at a fast-food place.

What's Cool: Funny situations, fantasy meets real life.

Vinland Saga

Plot: A Viking boy seeks revenge, learning about war, honor, and self-discovery.

What's Cool: Brutal battles, historical setting, and deep character growth.

My Hero Academia

Plot: In a world where most people have superpowers (quirks), a powerless boy aspires to become the greatest hero.

What's Cool: Unique hero abilities, epic training, and heartwarming friendships.

One Punch Man

Plot: A hero who can defeat any enemy with one punch grows bored and looks for worthy challenges.

What's Cool: Comedy, overpowered fights, and clever takes on superhero tropes.

Wistoria: Wand and Sword

Plot: In a world where magic and swordsmanship coexist, a young boy embarks on a journey to become a mage-knight.

What's Cool: Magic, adventure, and a mix of sword fights with fantasy themes.
